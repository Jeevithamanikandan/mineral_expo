About the Event

This exhibition & workshop aims to showcase the mineral wealth and mining ecosystem of Andhra Pradesh. The event brings together academia, industry and government for knowledge exchange, demonstration of mining equipment, sample displays and hands-on training sessions for students and early-career researchers.
